Our Services

Tyre Repair

The health of your tyres is critical to your safety on the road. Which is why we offer tyre services that keep you and your passengers secure. Stop by us to fix any tyre leaks, and have your tyres rotated and balanced. Doing so prevents uneven wear and increases the longevity of your tires.

Wheel Alignment

An out-of-alignment vehicle pulls and drifts from a straight road, causing excessive tyre wear and poor traction. Vehicle manufacturers recommend performing a wheel alignment every 6 months. Bring in your vehicle and our alignment experts will make sure your vehicle drives smoothly and safely.

Wheel Balancing

Tyre balancing ensures that the tyre weight is evenly distributed, and reduces vibrations of your vehicle at faster speeds. Well balanced tyres will help your vehicle to run smoothly, and improves the lifespan of tyres. We perform routine maintenance to ensure that your tyres are properly balanced.
